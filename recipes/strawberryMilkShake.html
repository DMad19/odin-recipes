Strawberry MilkShake

Description

Strawberry MilkShake is one of the yummy ,tasty MilkShake that can be served cool for the better taste. It's will just water some mouths . PS: As you know ,These words are just for educational purpose.When it's summer , imagine having a creamy,tasty,yummy strawberry MilkShake under the hot sun. We can say it's so tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound fresh strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pint vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Small whole strawberries, garnish

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Cut the tops off the strawberries and slice them into a few pieces.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine the sliced strawberries, sugar, and vanilla extract and stir to combine well.
  4. Set aside and allow to sit for at least 20 minutes and for up to 1 hour.
  5. Place the strawberries with any juices, ice cream, and milk in a blender.
  6. Blend until smooth.
  7. Pour into large glasses and, if desired, put a strawberry on the rim of each glass.
